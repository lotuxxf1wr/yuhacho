Yuha Cho is a graphic and visual designer, specialized in brand identity, editorial design, web design and visual direction. She initially studied journalism in South Korea and has continued her design practice after moving to New York. She enjoys the subversion of materials, seamlessly navigating between digital and print, contemporary and analog realms. She was previously at No Ideas, currently pursuing BFA Design at School of Visual Arts.
